Module 1: Hardware (Part 1)
Chapter 1: What is a Computer?
Learning Objectives:

Recognize what a computer is
Identify computers in everyday life
Understand that computers help us do many things

Topics:

What does a computer look like?
Computers are our smart helpers
Where do we see computers? (home, school, shops)
Fun activity: Spot the computer (picture identification)


Chapter 2: Parts of a Computer We Can See
Learning Objectives:

Identify the main external parts of a computer
Learn the names: monitor, keyboard, mouse, CPU
Understand what each part looks like

Topics:

The Monitor - Our computer's TV screen
The Keyboard - Where we press buttons with letters
The Mouse - Our computer's moving friend
The CPU/System Unit - The computer's box
Fun activity: Label the computer parts


Chapter 3: The Monitor - Our Window to the Computer
Learning Objectives:

Understand what a monitor does
Recognize different types of screens
Learn to care for the monitor

Topics:

The monitor shows us pictures and words
Big screens and small screens
Touching screens (tablets and phones)
Taking care of our monitor (no touching, gentle cleaning)
Fun activity: Draw what you see on a screen


Chapter 4: The Keyboard - Let's Type!
Learning Objectives:

Identify the keyboard
Recognize letters and numbers on keys
Understand basic keyboard use

Topics:

Keys with letters A to Z
Keys with numbers 0 to 9
Special keys (Space bar, Enter key - keep it simple!)
Pressing keys gently
Fun activity: Find your name's letters on the keyboard


Chapter 5: The Mouse - Point and Click
Learning Objectives:

Learn how to hold a mouse
Understand clicking
Practice moving the mouse pointer

Topics:

What does a mouse look like?
How to hold the mouse
The left button - for clicking
Moving the pointer on screen
Mice with wires and without wires
Fun activity: Mouse movement games


Chapter 6: The CPU - The Computer's Brain Box
Learning Objectives:

Identify the CPU/system unit
Understand it's an important part (simplified)
Recognize the power button

Topics:

The big box (or small box in laptops)
This is where the computer thinks
The power button - turning computer on/off
Ports and holes - where we plug things in
Fun activity: Find the power button


Chapter 7: Other Computer Parts We Use
Learning Objectives:

Identify additional computer parts
Understand speakers, printers, webcams (basic level)

Topics:

Speakers - The computer talks and plays music
Printer - The computer draws on paper
Webcam - The computer's camera
Headphones - For listening quietly
Fun activity: Match the part to what it does


Chapter 8: Laptops and Tablets - Computers We Can Carry
Learning Objectives:

Recognize laptops and tablets as computers
Understand they have the same parts built-in
Learn about portable computers

Topics:

Laptop computers - Fold and carry
All parts in one (monitor, keyboard together)
Tablets - Touch screen computers
Using computers anywhere
Fun activity: Desktop vs Laptop comparison


Chapter 9: Taking Care of Our Computer
Learning Objectives:

Learn basic computer care
Understand gentle handling
Practice good computer habits

Topics:

Clean hands before using computer
No food or water near the computer
Gentle touching (no banging on keyboard)
Asking an adult for help
Turning off properly
Fun activity: Good habits vs bad habits sorting game
